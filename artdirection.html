Art Direction

What is Art Direction

Art Direction -- is the primary focus and message that a composition is designed to convey, even if the composition is cropped or adjusted to fit a particular screen size.

  • What is the message of this painting by artist John Scott?

  • Is the focus of the message clear?

  • What design elements bring this composition together?

Core principles of art & design apply to most forms of media message communication.

Responsive Web Design

The world views digital content on different sized screens. Responsive web design means that content, such as images and videos, will scale to fit the screen size.
